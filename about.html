Greetings! My name is Will, as you can see above! I am 24 years old, and I am from the United States. Delaware Specifically. I originally went to school for Computer Aided Drafting & Design, and somehow along that road, found my way into Sales. After spending about the last 5 years working in Sales, I am quite tired of the process, and looking for something remote, and still something that makes me think. I find that Software Development checks a lot of boxes for me. I can be a introvert when I want, and extrovert when I need to be.

When I am not "fun-jumping" for my own enjoyment, I like to video-tape other people skydiving, and I like to do aerial photography. (Why not right?)
